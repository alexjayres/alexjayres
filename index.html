What we do

Understand your needs

We'll work with you to understand what you're looking for in your first PM and tailor our interview to make sure we find you a great match.

An independent perspective

Recruitment firms are great for finding candidates, but are incentivised mainly on filling your role. We'll provide an independent perspective.

Detailed feedback

For each candidate we interview for you, we'll send you detailed feedback. Hopefully we'll confirm your candidate is great hire, but if not, you can use this to work with your recruiter to improve the candidates they are sharing with you.

Competitive market

The PM market is competitive. We've seen for ourselves how the best PM candidates can get hired within days of coming to market so you'll need to move fast to find the best candidates. We will aim to interview your potential hire and share feedback within 24 hours.
